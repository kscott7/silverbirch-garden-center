About Silverbirch

Silverbirch is a third generation independent Garden Centre & Coffee Shop established in 1976 by Ronald Wood. Situated in Scotland’s picturesque Clyde Valley, Silverbirch is renowned for offering high quality plants and homeware at excellent prices.

As Scotland’s most design led garden centre, Silverbirch takes pride in interior design and attention to detail. No one does Christmas quite like Silverbirch.
