About Us

Silverbirch is a third generation independent Garden Centre & Coffee Shop established in 1976 by Ronald Wood. Situated in Scotland’s picturesque Clyde Valley, Silverbirch is renowned for offering high quality plants and homeware at excellent prices. As Scotland’s most design led garden centre, Silverbirch takes pride in interior design and attention to detail. No one does Christmas quite like Silverbirch.

We primarily support Scottish growers and only source the best plants from the UK as well as abroad. Plants are delivered daily to ensure the best possible quality for you.

Our team of staff are always here to help.
