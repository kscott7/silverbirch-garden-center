Garden

At Silverbirch our two main objectives are price and quality. This has enabled us to build an outstanding reputation from both customers and suppliers alike.

We primarily support Scottish growers but to always ensure great quality, we also source plants from the UK as well as abroad. Plants are delivered daily to ensure the best possible quality for you.

In the Garden

Shrubs
We have a vast range of typical shrubs as well as more exotic varieties. These are part of an all year round promotional offer 2 for £10.99 – choose from over 80 varieties.

Hardy Perennials
Perennial flowering plants that come up year after year. On offer 4 for £12.00.

Conifers
Forms take in columnular, prostrate and many other shapes. Most are winter hardy and considered a very reliable evergreen. One of the best hedging plants in Europe.

Trees
At Silverbirch we believe that every garden should have at least one tree. We have trees to suit every space – be it very small or large, sunny or shaded.

Herbs
Everyone can grow their own. There’s no time like the present. Herbs, salads, tomatoes & veg are available in season.

Roses
Britain’s favourite flower. Hybrid tea, floribunda, shrub and climbing roses available in season.

Topiary
Box balls and pyramids are stylish, reliable and evergreen. We have many sizes available. Large specimens are available to order – delivery usually within one week. 2 for £40.00.

Bedding
We have many weekly offers on bedding plants. 95% grown locally for you which means fresh colour every day. Patio/hanging basket plants, fuschias, geraniums and trailing petunias all available.

Wild Birdcare

Encourage and support nature in your garden with our range of bird care products. Our quality seeds, seed mixes and treats will tempt an array of feathered friends into your garden. Enjoy the beauty of wild birds at home. Bring your garden to life.
