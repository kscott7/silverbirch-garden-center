Interiors

Silverbirch Interiors and Homeware department believe that home will always be the place we turn to for a sense of calm and security.

We offer a selection of timeless classics to give your home that special touch. This season choose just a handful of beautiful, luxurious, practical and versatile ‘must haves’ to make your home perfect. We strive to source a high quality of homeware and aim to offer great value for money.

We specialise in scented candles & reeds as well as console tables, mirrors, glassware, ornamental pieces, cushions and occasional chairs.
